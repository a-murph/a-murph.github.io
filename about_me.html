About Me

Andrew is a full stack web developer. He is a recent graduate and substitute teaching assistant for the Rutgers Web Development Program. His experience in programming coupled with skills in customer service allows for him to work with a team oriented, user first mindset.

Andrew is fueled by his passion for understanding the nuances of how computers interact with each other. He considers himself a ‘forever student,’ eager to both build on his academic foundations in website development, and stay in tune with the latest technologies being developed to improve both user and developer experience.

He recently has successfully collaborated with a team on projects such as a personal movie goer’s review/watch list website, that utilizes technologies such as MongoDB, Express, React, Node and the OMBD api. He has also worked on projects such as website scrapers, and a functional clone of the popular social media platform Instagram.
